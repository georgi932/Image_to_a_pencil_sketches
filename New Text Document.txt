# 3 допълнителни функционалности

Image Filters:
Implement various image filters to enhance or stylize images. Examples of filters include blur, sharpen, edge detection, emboss, sepia tone, and grayscale conversion. Users can apply these filters to their images to achieve different artistic effects or to improve image quality.

Image Segmentation:
Implement image segmentation algorithms to partition an image into multiple segments or regions based on attributes such as color, intensity, or texture. Users can use segmentation to extract objects or regions of interest from an image, perform object recognition, or analyze the composition of an image.

Image Restoration:
Implement techniques for image restoration to remove noise, artifacts, or imperfections from images. This could involve methods such as denoising, deblurring, inpainting, or super-resolution. Users can use these techniques to improve the quality and clarity of their images, especially those that are degraded or low-resolution.
